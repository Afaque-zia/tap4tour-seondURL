Customer Complaint & Grievance Redressal Policy

At Tap4Tour, customer satisfaction and trust are our top priorities. We strive to provide exceptional travel services, and we take complaints seriously. This page outlines how customers can raise concerns and how those concerns will be addressed in a timely and fair manner.

How to File a Complaint

To ensure proper handling and documentation, please choose one of the official channels below:

A. Online Submission (Preferred)

Fill out the official complaint form on our website including the following details:
Full Name
Registered Mobile Number & Email
Booking ID or Reference Number (if applicable)
Detailed Description of the Complaint
Supporting documents/screenshots (optional but helpful).

B. Email Support

Email your complaint to grievance@tap4tour.com with all necessary details.
